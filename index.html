The Local Delicacy, "Fufu"

Fufu is a Ghanaian Dish that is widely eaten across the nation

Ingredients List

  • 300g un-ripened Plantain
  • 350-450ml(1 1/2 cup to 2 cups- 1tbsp)water for blending
  • 50ml (3tbsp + 1tsp) water for steaming

Steps in making the Fufu

  1. Place the unpeeled casava in a large pot, cover with cold water and bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  2. Boil for 15 to 30 minutes, or until the casava are cooked through and tender
  3. Drain the water and let let cool somewhat
  4. Peel the Casava, chop them into large pieces and place them into a large bowl with the butter, salt and pepper
  5. Masha with a potato masher until very smooth
  6. Placethe fufu into a large serving bowl. Wet your hands with water, form into a large ball and serve
